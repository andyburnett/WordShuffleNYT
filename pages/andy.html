Andy's Year in Pictures

Duo

On our way to Austria, Cyndi downloaded the Duolingo app. She thought it would be a good idea to learn to speak German. We had a few hours on the flight, so we both thought it would reasonably easy to become moderately fluent. As it happens, German is a bit more complicated than we expected! However, our exposure to Duolingo and its very very clever gamification of learning has resulted in us both becoming rather obsessed with keeping our streaks going.

Sondheim

I love Les Miserables. We had an extra day in London, and we were able to get tickets for the production at the Sondheim Theatre in the West End. If you happen find yourself in London, we cannot recommend it highly enough. Quite astonishing.

Ithaca

The time has come to start looking at universities for James. We weren't sure where to start, but James's old mathematics tutor was now at Cornell, so we thought we would take a trip to Ithaca, and tour the university.

I would imagine that Cornell can look a bit uninspiring on a rainy day in February, but when we went it was an absolutely perfect autumnal day. It is the sort day that university recruiters long for, and it is fair to say that we were impressed!

On-The-Lake

Niagara-on-the-Lake is beautiful. It also has some fabulous restaurants. So, we decided to rent a cottage for a few days, and enjoy the combination of the scenary and the food. Perfect getaway.
